== Description ==

_You can add only one link to your profile._

WP Bio Links is a free plugin designed to help you organize traffic from your profile. Create a customizable landing page that you can add to your bio profile. The landing page holds all the content you wish to share with your followers: articles, newsletters, website links, shops, social media and more so that you can drive all traffic from bio profiles directly to your website.

= Benefits =
* Never change your link bio again
* Using your own domain increases the value of your brand
* Drive 100% of profile's traffic directly to your website

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Why do I need this? =

Most of the popular social media and services sites allow only a single URL in your profile. Think Instagram, Twitter or Pinterest for example. Let's say you have a personal website, a shop page, other social profiles and a cool new article. Because of the one link policy, you have to pick and choose which link to share on your bio page.

= Is mobile-friendly? =

Yes! WP Bio Links plugin you can create a simple, mobile-friendly profile landing page and house all your other links there. Now instead of you choosing where to lead your followers, each person can choose what they want to see next. Connect all your links and profiles on one page to drive more traffic.

= How it works? =
Bio Links will allow you to create as many link landing pages as you need. Each landing page will have its own unique address, nickname and as many links as you need. The profile picture, the background and link color is customizable and they are common for all landing pages. 

= Can I request new feature? =
Of course! I love to get your ideas! Please, [submit your idea](https://wordpress.org/support/plugin/wp-bio-links/) here.

= Is speed? =
WP Bio Links is optimized for speed, it won't load your theme files. The plugin will load its own styles and template files to maximize the performance of your landing pages! Give it a try and send your followers to content that deserves their attention.

= How to add links? =
1. Go to WP Bio Links > Add New
1. Add landing page name
1. Add your profile nickname if exists 
1. Add text and URL of the link
1. Hit "Publish"

= How to reorder links? =
You can reorder links with apposite buttons in link box. You can also delete link.


== Installation ==
1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/wp-bio-links` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
1. Use the WP Bio Links->Settings screen to configure the plugin
